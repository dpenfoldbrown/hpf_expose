About

The HPF project, or Human Proteome Folding project, is an effort to classify protein domains and predict 3-dimensional protein structure from sequence alone. It is the babychild of Dr. Richard Bonneau, currently upheld by Dr. Bonneau's research group at the NYU CGSB.
